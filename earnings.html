Core Disciplines of Accounting

The accounting industry branches into distinct paths. Financial Accounting involves organizing structural records for external parties like shareholders and banks. Managerial Accounting builds internal data strategies to help managers run daily workflows. Tax Accounting ensures legally sound filings with Inland Revenue (IRD), and Auditing objectively verifies fiscal accuracy.
